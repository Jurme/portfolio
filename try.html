UX Case Study

NewRentals

The NewRentals is an all-in-one real estate property and rental management system and rental marketplace for people who want to find their next home and list properties for rent. It aims to automate the process of renting, onboarding people to properties, and managing the tenants by the property owners.

Problem Statement

In real estate, there's a need for a simple system that combines property rental management and a rental marketplace. Without this system, people struggle to find homes to rent easily, and property owners have a hard time managing their rentals and tenants. Traditional methods involve a lot of manual work and inefficiencies, causing delays and mistakes. This creates frustration for everyone involved. Therefore, a system that automates the rental process, makes it easy to list properties, and helps owners manage tenants efficiently is in high demand.
