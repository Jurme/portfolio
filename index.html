About me

I am Jurme Tenzin, Senior UX UI Designer based in Bhutan with 5 years of experience, specializing in end-to-end user-centered design with a passion for creating digital products that are seamless and impactful.

My primary objective is to assist in accomplishing and maximizing business goals and key performance indicators (KPIs) by providing optimal design solutions that align with the specific needs of the business, taking into account context, content, and user considerations.
