Problem Statement

Property owners and tenants face significant challenges in the rental process, including inefficient property listings, time-consuming tenant screening, and complex document management. Research

We conducted extensive user interviews with both property owners and tenants to understand their pain points and needs.

Key Insights

  • Manual document management leads to disorganization
  • Lack of standardized rental processes causes confusion
  • Communication gaps between owners and tenants
