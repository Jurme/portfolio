Problem Statement

In the realm of real estate, there exists a pressing need for a comprehensive solution that seamlessly integrates property rental management and a user-friendly rental marketplace. The absence of such a system creates challenges for individuals seeking a streamlined approach to finding suitable homes for rent, as well as for property owners in efficiently onboarding tenants and managing their properties. Traditional methods of renting properties and managing tenants are fraught with manual processes, inefficiencies, and fragmented tools, leading to time-consuming tasks, potential errors, and frustrated stakeholders. Consequently, there is a clear demand for a solution that automates the rental process, facilitates seamless property onboarding, and empowers property owners to effortlessly manage tenants.

User and Audience

Property Owners

These are individuals who own rental properties and seek effective ways to efficiently onboard tenants, manage their properties, and address various challenges related to tenant management.

Tenants/Individuals searching for homes to rent

This includes people who are searching for suitable homes to rent or who are currently renting properties.

Design Process

Discovery Phase

Qualitative Research - User Interviews

To recruit interview participants, I conducted a screening survey to represent a diverse demographic and user group of property owners and tenants or people looking for the next home. This helped to conduct two sets of interviews with property owners and tenants or people who want to find new homes. We conducted phone interviews with 6 property owners and 7 tenants to gain insights into the difficulties they encounter when it comes to listing properties for rent and the processes involved in o nboarding and overseeing tenants.

Property Owners

A selection of example questions is provided below.

When you consider listing a property for rent, what are the most time-consuming or complex steps you encounter?

Could you describe your process of onboarding new tenants? What pain points have you encountered during this process?

What tools or methods do you currently use to manage tenant communication and address their concerns or requests?

How do you handle the maintenance and repair needs of your rental properties? Are there any challenges in coordinating these tasks?

How do you currently screen potential tenants? Are there any specific aspects of tenant screening that you find difficult to manage?

In your experience, what key features or functionalities would you consider essential in a platform that aims to simplify property listing, tenant onboarding, and management tasks?

Tenants and people looking for next home to stay

A selection of example questions is provided below.

Could you describe your typical process for finding and booking a rental property? What are the main factors you consider?

What challenges or frustrations have you encountered when searching for rental properties online? Is there anything you find confusing or time-consuming?

Can you walk me through the steps you take to schedule a viewing or visit a property you're interested in? Are there any difficulties you face during this process?

What information do you find most important to have access to when considering a rental property? Is this information typically available in your searches?

Can you describe your experience with reporting maintenance issues or repair requests to your landlord or property management? Are there any challenges you've faced?

How do you typically communicate with your landlord or property manager about questions or concerns related to your living situation? Is this communication effective?
